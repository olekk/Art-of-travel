Thessaloniki

City considered to be Greece's cultural capital. Lies on the Aegean Sea, so after sightseeing you can just relax on the beach.

Notable places in Thessaloniki

  • Theatro Dasous
  • Garden park at Nea Paralia
  • Statue of Alexander the Great
  • Archaeological Museum of Thessaloniki
  • Ruins of the Roman Forum
