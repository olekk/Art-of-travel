Zakopane

Something for moutain lovers. One of the most beautiful polish places. This town never sleeps. In the winter you will get so many possibilities for skiing or other winter sports and in summer there are a lot of places to go moutain hiking.

Places worth to go in Zakopane:

  • Giewont Hill
  • Old Zakopane Cemetery
  • Krupowki street
  • Gubalowka Hill
  • Morskie Oko

Zakopane is piece of history too. Because of its history, landscape and kind people there, it is really worth to visit. You will appriciate this place!
