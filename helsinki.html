Helsinki

Beautiful city in the south of Finland, which is located nearly Baltic Sea and a lot of lakes. Perfect place if you prefer relax at the water.

Beside lakes and sea you can find among others:

  • Helsinki Cathedral
  • Kiasma Museum
  • Suomenlinna
  • Finnish National Opera

Helsinki and whole Finland are so well-kept places. Air is very fresh there so it is good to run away from smoke and polltuion.
