Moscow

capital of Russia, but moreover capital of Architecture in eastern Europe. That is fact that people visiting this place with pleasure.

A few of visitable places in Moscow:

  • Red square:
    • Saint Basil's Cathedral
    • Kazan Cathedral
    • State Historical Museum
    • Kremlin towers
  • Pushkin Museum of Fine Arts
  • Memorial Museum of Astronautics
  • Luzhniki Stadium

In summary, you should visit Moscow if you are into history and great buildings makes you impressed. Don't think so much. Just come!
