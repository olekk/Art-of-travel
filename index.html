About us

Art of TRAVEL is a website which aims to curious people on holidays who want to experience diffrent activities than just sunbathing or boring walking through city without a target. By choosing a city you want to explore you will recieve informations about the best places to see, best attractions - cheap or more expensive. Dresden

is city with really deep history, so you can see there a piece of great architecture. Good for you if you prefer visiting places with amazing buildings.

Dubrovnik

is a Croatian city on the Adriatic Sea. It is known due to its incredible landscape, which is combination of sea and moutains. Perfect place to go on summer.

Helsinki

is perfect place for you if you love relax near water. Welcome in the capital of the land of a thousand lakes!

London

one of the most popular cities on the world. You can admire a lot of iconic places of the capital of Great Britain

Madrid

warm, relaxing place especially for architecture lovers. Better if you don't mind huge cities. It is one of the biggest in Europe.

Moscow

in this city you will see some of the greatest monuments of world architecture. Those will make you really impressed.

Paris

definitely one of the most beautiful cities in the world. We are inviting you especially with your partner.

capital of Italy. This place offers to see so many historical objects. Coloseum, pantheon or forum romanum are only a few of all of these admirable monumental places
